Charmeleon (Japanese: リザード Lizardo) is a Fire-type Stage 1 Pokémon card. It was first released as part of the Boundaries Crossed expansion.

Release information

This card was included in the English Boundaries Crossed expansion, first released in the Japanese Freeze Bolt, with artwork by Masakazu Fukuda. It was later reprinted with different artwork by Akira Komayama in the English Legendary Treasures expansion, originating from the Japanese EX Battle Boost.

Trivia

The Legendary Treasures print is one of numerous cards to feature an illustration that is part of a narrative. In this illustration, a Charmeleon is accidentally burning a Swablu's wing with the flame on its tail. The scene continues on the Swablu card.

Origin

This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Black 2 and White 2.
